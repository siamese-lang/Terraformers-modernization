# ADR-0012: Do Not Productize into a Platform Portal

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Terraformers could be misinterpreted as an environment request portal, Terraform approval platform, or Backstage/Service Catalog alternative.

## Decision

Do not productize Terraformers modernization into a platform portal. Keep the original service function and focus the follow-up work on backend and cloud infrastructure operations.

## Consequences

- The project avoids becoming a lower-quality clone of existing platform products.
- The modernization story remains credible for junior backend/cloud infrastructure roles.
- Runbooks and validation are framed as operations material, not platform product features.
